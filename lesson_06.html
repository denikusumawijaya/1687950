Algebra Review

Before we dive into geometry, let's refresh our algebra skills. Algebra is the language of mathematics used to represent unknown values with variables. Understanding how to construct and interpret expressions is crucial for solving complex geometric problems.

Sebelum kita menyelami geometri, mari segarkan keterampilan aljabar kita. Aljabar adalah bahasa matematika yang digunakan untuk mewakili nilai yang tidak diketahui dengan variabel. Memahami cara menyusun dan menafsirkan ekspresi sangat penting untuk memecahkan masalah geometri yang kompleks.

Quick-Fire Challenge
1. If 'x' is the price of a book, how to write '3 books are 5000 more expensive'? (Jika 'x' harga buku, tuliskan '3 buku lebih mahal 5000')
Solution: 3 books = 3x. More expensive by 5000 means add 5000. So, \( 3x + 5000 \).
2. Identify the constant in \(-4a + 12\). (Sebutkan konstanta dari -4a + 12)
Solution: A constant is a term without a variable. Here, it is 12.
3. Convert "Twice the age decreased by 3" into algebra. (Ubah 'dua kali umur dikurangi 3' jadi aljabar)
Solution: Twice the age = 2x. Decreased by 3 = - 3. Result: \( 2x - 3 \).
4. How many variables are in \(2x + 3y - 5\)? (Berapa variabel pada ekspresi 2x + 3y - 5?)
Solution: The variables are x and y. So there are 2 variables.
5. What is the coefficient of \(x\) in \(5 - 2x + y\)? (Koefisien x pada 5 - 2x + y?)
Solution: The term with x is -2x. Therefore, the coefficient is -2.

The Foundation of Geometry

Do you remember the basic formulas from elementary school? The area of a rectangle is Length times Width (\( L = p \times l \)), and the perimeter is the sum of all sides (\( K = 2(p + l) \)). For a square, since all sides are equal (\( s \)), Area is \( s^2 \) and Perimeter is \( 4s \). But today, we level up! What if the length is \( (x + 5) \)? We will merge algebra into geometry.

Ingat rumus dasar di SD? Luas persegi panjang adalah Panjang dikali Lebar (\( L = p \times l \)), dan Keliling adalah jumlah semua sisi (\( K = 2(p + l) \)). Tapi hari ini, kita naik level. Bagaimana jika panjangnya adalah \( (x + 5) \)? Kita akan menggabungkan aljabar ke dalam geometri.

Algebraic Expansion in Geometry

Look at this challenge: A rectangular garden has a length of \(2x\) meters and a width of \((x - 3)\) meters. If \(x = 10\), the area is \(2(10) \times (10 - 3) = 20 \times 7 = 140\) m². But what if we don't know \(x\)? We construct an algebraic expression using the distributive property: \( L = 2x(x - 3) = 2x^2 - 6x \).

Lihat tantangan ini: Sebuah taman berbentuk persegi panjang dengan panjang \(2x\) meter dan lebar \((x - 3)\) meter. Jika \(x = 10\), luasnya adalah 140 m². Bagaimana jika kita tidak tahu \(x\)? Kita membuat ekspresi aljabar menggunakan sifat distributif: \( L = 2x(x - 3) = 2x^2 - 6x \).

Finding Unknown Sides

Sometimes you know the perimeter but not the width. Let's say a rectangle has a length of \(3y\) and a perimeter of \(20y\). Remember: \( K = 2(p + l) \). Don't rush! First, divide the perimeter by 2 to get the sum of \( p + l \). So, \(10y = 3y + l\). Then, subtract the length from that sum to find the width!

Kadang kamu tahu keliling tapi tidak tahu lebar. Misalnya persegi panjang punya panjang \(3y\) dan keliling \(20y\). Ingat: \( K = 2(p + l) \). Jangan terburu-buru, bagi dua dulu kelilingnya agar dapat \( p + l \). Jadi, \(10y = 3y + l\). Lalu, kurangi panjang dari jumlah itu untuk menemukan lebar!

Hard Level Missions
1. A rectangle has \( p = (2x + 10) \) cm and \( l = x \) cm. If the perimeter is 80 cm, what is \( x \)? (Persegi panjang p=(2x+10) dan l=x. Jika K=80, berapa x?)
Solution: \( K = 2(2x+10+x) = 2(3x+10) = 6x+20 \). So \( 6x+20 = 80 \rightarrow 6x = 60 \rightarrow x = 10 \).
2. A garden is \( (3a) \) m long and \( (2a - 5) \) m wide. If \( a = 4 \), calculate the difference between Perimeter and Area! (Kebun p=3a, l=2a-5. Jika a=4, hitung selisih K dan L!)
Solution: \( a=4 \rightarrow p=12, l=3 \). \( K = 2(12+3) = 30 \). \( L = 12 \times 3 = 36 \). Wait, \( K = 30 \)? Let's recalculate: \( K = 2(12+3) = 30 \). \( L = 36 \). Difference = 6. Or \( K = 2(3(4) + 2(4)-5) = 2(12+3) = 30 \). \( L = 12 \times 3 = 36 \). Difference = 6. (Wait, the answer key says K=34, L=36, diff=2. Let's check: \(2(3a + 2a - 5) = 2(10a - 5) = 20a - 10\). If a=4, K=70. Ah, the key says K=34, L=36. Wait, if p=12, l=3, K=30. I'll adjust the options).
3. Rectangle A has \( p=12 \), \( l=8 \). If the length is increased by 25% and the width is decreased by 25%, is the new area the same? (Persegi panjang A p=12, l=8. Jika p ditambah 25% dan l dikurangi 25%, apakah luas tetap sama?)
Solution: Initial Area = \( 12 \times 8 = 96 \). New \( p = 12 \times 1.25 = 15 \). New \( l = 8 \times 0.75 = 6 \). New Area = \( 15 \times 6 = 90 \). It decreases by 6 cm².

Group 1: Geometry Basics (Dasar Geometri)

1. What is the perimeter of a square with side \( 5 \) cm? (Keliling persegi sisi 5 cm?)
Solution: \( K = 4 \times s = 4 \times 5 = 20 \) cm.
2. The formula for the area of a rectangle is... (Rumus luas persegi panjang adalah...)
Solution: Area of rectangle is Length times Width (\( L = p \times l \)).
3. A rectangle is \( 8 \) cm long and \( 4 \) cm wide. Calculate its perimeter! (Keliling persegi panjang 8x4 cm?)
Solution: \( K = 2(8 + 4) = 2(12) = 24 \) cm.
4. Which of the following are properties of a square? (Select MORE THAN ONE) (Manakah sifat persegi? Pilih lebih dari 1)
Solution: A square has all sides equal, all angles 90°, and equal diagonals.
5. If the side of a square is doubled, its area will be... (Jika sisi persegi digandakan, luasnya akan...)
Solution: New area = \( (2s)^2 = 4s^2 \). It becomes 4 times the original.
6. The perimeter of a square is \( 20 \) cm. What is its side length? (Keliling persegi 20 cm, berapa sisinya?)
Solution: \( s = K / 4 = 20 / 4 = 5 \) cm.
7. The correct unit for area is... (Satuan yang benar untuk luas adalah...)
Solution: Area is measured in square units, like cm².
8. Find the area of a square with side \( 6 \) cm. (Luas persegi sisi 6 cm?)
Solution: \( L = 6 \times 6 = 36 \) cm².
9. Which shapes have 4 right angles? (Select MORE THAN ONE) (Bangun mana yang memiliki 4 sudut siku-siku?)
Solution: Rectangles and Squares both have four 90° angles.
10. A rectangle is \( 10 \) cm long and \( 5 \) cm wide. What is its perimeter? (Keliling persegi panjang 10x5 cm?)
Solution: \( K = 2(10 + 5) = 2(15) = 30 \) cm.

Group 2: Algebra in Perimeter (Aljabar pada Keliling)

1. A rectangle has \( p = 2x \) and \( l = x \). What is its perimeter? (Keliling persegi panjang p=2x, l=x?)
Solution: \( K = 2(2x + x) = 2(3x) = 6x \).
2. If \( K = 10y \) and width is \( 2y \), what is the length? (Jika K=10y dan l=2y, berapa p?)
Solution: \( p+l = 5y \). So \( p = 5y - 2y = 3y \).
3. Perimeter of a square with side \( 3a \) is... (Keliling persegi sisi 3a adalah...)
Solution: \( K = 4 \times 3a = 12a \).
4. Which expressions correctly represent the perimeter of a rectangle with \( p = x \) and \( l = x+2 \)? (Select MORE THAN ONE) (Ekspresi mana yang benar untuk K?)
Solution: \( 2(x + x + 2) = 2(2x+2) = 4x+4 \). Option A is the direct formula, B is expanded. C is Area.
5. Perimeter of a square with side \( (a+1) \) is... (Keliling persegi sisi (a+1)?)
Solution: \( K = 4(a+1) = 4a + 4 \).
6. A rectangle has \( p=3x \), \( l=x \), and \( K=24 \). Find \( x \). (Tentukan x jika p=3x, l=x, K=24)
Solution: \( 2(3x+x) = 24 \rightarrow 8x = 24 \rightarrow x = 3 \).
7. A rectangle has length \( x \) and width \( 5 \). Its perimeter is... (Keliling p=x, l=5?)
Solution: \( K = 2(x+5) = 2x + 10 \).
8. If \( p=2x, l=x, K=12 \), what is \( x \)? (Jika p=2x, l=x, K=12, berapa x?)
Solution: \( 2(2x+x) = 12 \rightarrow 6x = 12 \rightarrow x=2 \).
9. Which formulas correctly calculate the perimeter of a rectangle? (Select MORE THAN ONE) (Rumus mana yang benar untuk keliling?)
Solution: All three are mathematically equivalent ways to write perimeter.
10. Evaluate the perimeter of a rectangle with \( p=2x, l=x \) if \( x=3 \). (Hitung K jika p=2x, l=x dan x=3)
Solution: \( K = 2(2x+x) = 6x \). If \( x=3, K = 6(3) = 18 \).

Group 3: Area Expressions (Ekspresi Luas)

1. What is the area of a rectangle with \( p=x \) and \( l=2 \)? (Luas persegi panjang p=x, l=2?)
Solution: \( L = p \times l = x \times 2 = 2x \).
2. Area of a square with side \( 2y \) is... (Luas persegi sisi 2y?)
Solution: \( L = (2y)^2 = 4y^2 \).
3. A rectangle has \( p=3x \) and \( l=x \). Express its Area. (Nyatakan Luas p=3x, l=x)
Solution: \( L = 3x \times x = 3x^2 \).
4. Which expressions are equivalent to \( 2x(x + 3) \)? (Select MORE THAN ONE) (Mana yang setara dengan 2x(x+3)?)
Solution: Distributing gives \( 2x^2 + 6x \), which is also \( 2(x^2 + 3x) \).
5. Area of a square with side \( (a-1) \) is... (Luas persegi sisi (a-1))
Solution: \( (a-1)^2 = a^2 - 2a + 1 \).
6. Find the area if \( p=2x, l=x+1 \) and \( x=4 \). (Hitung luas jika p=2x, l=x+1, x=4)
Solution: \( p=8, l=5 \). \( L = 8 \times 5 = 40 \).
7. Expand \( x(4x - 5) \). (Ekspansi x(4x - 5))
Solution: Distribute \( x \): \( 4x^2 - 5x \).
8. If \( p=2x \) and \( l=3x \), what is the area? (Luas jika p=2x, l=3x?)
Solution: \( L = 2x \times 3x = 6x^2 \).
9. Which represent the Area of a square? (Select MORE THAN ONE) (Mana yang mewakili Luas Persegi?)
Solution: Area is side times side (\( s \times s \) or \( s^2 \)). \( 4s \) is perimeter.
10. Difference between Area of square (\( s=x \)) and rectangle (\( p=x, l=2 \)). (Selisih luas persegi s=x dan pp p=x, l=2)
Solution: Square area = \( x^2 \). Rect area = \( 2x \). Difference = \( x^2 - 2x \).

Group 4: Contextual Problems (Masalah Kontekstual)

1. A garden is \( (3a) \) m long and \( (2a - 5) \) m wide. If \( a = 4 \), calculate its Area! (Kebun p=3a, l=2a-5. Jika a=4, hitung Luas!)
Solution: \( a=4 \rightarrow p=12, l=3 \). \( L = 12 \times 3 = 36 \) m².
2. Rectangle A has \( p=12, l=8 \). If length +25% and width -25%, does the Area stay the same? (Apakah luas tetap sama?)
Solution: New \( p = 15 \), new \( l = 6 \). New Area = \( 15 \times 6 = 90 \). It decreases.
3. A square has side \( 4x \) cm. What is its perimeter? (Keliling persegi sisi 4x?)
Solution: \( K = 4 \times 4x = 16x \) cm.

Group 5: Advanced Logic (Logika Lanjutan)

1. Two squares have sides \( s_1 \) and \( s_2 \). If \( s_1 = 2 \times s_2 \), how does Area 1 compare to Area 2? (Perbandingan luas?)
Solution: \( L_1 = (2s_2)^2 = 4s_2^2 \). Since \( L_2 = s_2^2 \), \( L_1 = 4 \times L_2 \).
2. Rectangle has \( p=12 \) cm, \( l=(y+2) \) cm. Express Area. (Nyatakan Luas dalam y)
Solution: \( L = 12(y+2) = 12y + 24 \).
3. Perimeter of a square equals Perimeter of a 10x6 rectangle. Area of the square is... (Luas persegi?)
Solution: \( K_{rect} = 32 \). \( s_{square} = 32/4 = 8 \). \( L = 8 \times 8 = 64 \).
