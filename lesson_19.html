1. Spiral Review Warm-up

1. Tinjauan Pengulangan

Theory 1: Algebra Basics

In algebra, a constant is a number on its own, a coefficient is a number multiplied by a variable, and a variable is a letter representing an unknown number.

Dalam aljabar, konstanta adalah angka tersendiri, koefisien adalah angka dikali variabel, dan variabel adalah huruf yang mewakili angka yang tidak diketahui.
1. What is the constant in the expression \( 5x + 7 - 2y \)?
Berapakah konstanta dari ekspresi tersebut?
2. What is the coefficient of \( y \) in \( 3x - 4y + 9 \)?
Berapakah koefisien dari y?
3. If 'p' is your game score, write the expression for '3 points less than twice your score'.
Tuliskan ekspresi untuk '3 poin lebih sedikit dari dua kali poinmu'.


Theory 2: Translating Words to Math

Real-world problems can be translated into algebraic expressions. "Total cost of \( x \) books at 5000 each and 1 pen at 2000" becomes \( 5000x + 2000 \).

Masalah dunia nyata dapat diterjemahkan ke dalam ekspresi aljabar. "Total harga x buku seharga 5000 dan satu pulpen 2000" menjadi \( 5000x + 2000 \).
4. Write the expression for "Total price of \( x \) books at 5000 each and 1 pen at 2000".
Tuliskan ekspresi untuk total harga tersebut.


5. Which expression represents "5 more than a number \( n \)"?
Ekspresi mana yang mewakili "5 lebihnya dari sebuah angka n"?
6. A burger costs \( b \). If you buy 3 burgers with a 2000 discount on each, what is the expression?
Harga burger b. Jika beli 3 burger masing-masing diskon 2000, apa ekspresinya?

Theory 3: Cartesian Coordinates

Points on a plane are given as \( (x, y) \). The x-axis is horizontal, and the y-axis is vertical.

Titik pada bidang diberikan sebagai (x, y). Sumbu x horizontal, sumbu y vertikal.
7. In the point \( (2, 3) \), what does 2 represent?
Pada titik (2, 3), apa yang diwakili oleh 2?
8. Which point lies on the y-axis?
Titik mana yang terletak pada sumbu y?
9. If you move from \( (1, 1) \) to \( (1, 4) \), how many units did you move vertically?
Jika bergerak dari (1,1) ke (1,4), berapa unit gerak vertikalnya?


10. If you move from \( (2, 3) \) to \( (5, 3) \), how many units did you move horizontally?
Berapa unit gerak horizontalnya?


2. Introduction & Real-World Connection

2. Pengenalan & Koneksi Dunia Nyata

Theory 1: What is Slope?

In games like Minecraft, stairs and roofs have a "tilt" or "steepness". In math, this is called the Slope (Gradien). If it's too steep, a roller coaster might crash; if too flat, it won't move.

Dalam game seperti Minecraft, tangga dan atap punya "kemiringan". Dalam matematika, ini disebut Gradien. Kalau terlalu miring kereta jatuh, kalau terlalu datar kereta tidak jalan.
1. In real life, which of these has the steepest slope?
Di dunia nyata, mana yang memiliki kemiringan paling curam?
2. A roller coaster needs to move forward. What kind of slope is ideal for the start?
Roller coaster perlu maju. Kemiringan seperti apa yang ideal untuk awal?
3. What do we call the "steepness" of a line in mathematics?
Apa yang kita sebut "kemiringan" garis di matematika?

Theory 2: Types of Slopes

A line can go up (positive), go down (negative), be flat (zero), or go straight up/down (undefined).

Garis bisa naik (positif), turun (negatif), datar (nol), atau vertikal (tidak terdefinisi).
4. If a line goes upwards from left to right, its slope is...
Jika garis naik dari kiri ke kanan, gradiennya adalah...
5. A horizontal flat line has a slope of...
Garis horizontal datar memiliki gradien...
6. If a line goes downwards from left to right, its slope is...
Jika garis turun dari kiri ke kanan, gradiennya adalah...

Theory 3: Rise over Run

Slope is calculated as the change in vertical position (Rise) divided by the change in horizontal position (Run).

Gradien dihitung sebagai perubahan posisi vertikal (Rise) dibagi perubahan posisi horizontal (Run).
7. In "Rise over Run", what does "Rise" refer to?
Pada "Rise over Run", apa arti "Rise"?
8. If the Rise is 4 and the Run is 2, what is the slope?
Jika Rise 4 dan Run 2, berapakah gradiennya?


9. If the Rise is -3 and the Run is 3, what is the slope?
Jika Rise -3 dan Run 3, berapakah gradiennya?


10. True or False: A larger "Run" than "Rise" means the slope is steep (greater than 1).
Benar atau Salah: Run yang lebih besar dari Rise berarti kemiringan curam.

3. Review of Prior Knowledge

3. Tinjauan Pengetahuan Sebelumnya

Theory 1: Cartesian Coordinates

To find slope, we must be fluent with Cartesian coordinates. Points are written as \( (x, y) \). The x-axis is horizontal, and the y-axis is vertical.

Untuk mencari gradien, kita harus lancar dengan koordinat Kartesius. Titik ditulis sebagai (x, y). Sumbu x horizontal, sumbu y vertikal.
1. In the point \( A(2, 3) \), what is the x-coordinate?
Pada titik A(2, 3), berapakah koordinat x-nya?
2. Plot point \( A(2, 3) \) mentally. If you move to point \( B(5, 9) \), how many units do you move horizontally (Run)?
Pindah dari A(2,3) ke B(5,9), berapa unit gerak horizontal (Run)?


3. Moving from \( A(2, 3) \) to \( B(5, 9) \), how many units do you move vertically (Rise)?
Berapa unit gerak vertikal (Rise)?


Theory 2: Rise and Run as Differences

The "Rise" is the difference in y-values: \( y_2 - y_1 \). The "Run" is the difference in x-values: \( x_2 - x_1 \). It's like finding the distance between two steps.

"Rise" adalah selisih nilai y: y2 - y1. "Run" adalah selisih nilai x: x2 - x1. Seperti mencari jarak antar dua anak tangga.
4. Given points \( P(1, 2) \) and \( Q(4, 6) \). Calculate the Rise (\( y_2 - y_1 \)).
Hitunglah Rise (y2 - y1).


5. For the same points \( P(1, 2) \) and \( Q(4, 6) \). Calculate the Run (\( x_2 - x_1 \)).
Hitunglah Run (x2 - x1).


6. If Rise is 5 and Run is 5, what fraction represents the slope conceptually?
Jika Rise 5 dan Run 5, berapa pecahan yang merepresentasikan gradien?

Theory 3: Connecting Rise/Run to Formula

Slope (\( m \)) is Rise divided by Run. This gives us the core formula: \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \).

Gradien (m) adalah Rise dibagi Run. Ini memberi kita rumus inti: m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1).
7. Using the formula \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \), plug in \( A(2, 3) \) and \( B(5, 9) \). What is the numerator (top)?
Menggunakan rumus, masukkan A(2,3) dan B(5,9). Berapakah pembilangnya?


8. What is the denominator (bottom) for the same points \( A(2, 3) \) and \( B(5, 9) \)?
Berapakah penyebutnya?


9. Calculate the slope \( m \) for \( A(2, 3) \) and \( B(5, 9) \).
Hitunglah gradien m.


10. If a line goes through \( (0,0) \) and \( (2,6) \), what is its slope?
Jika garis melalui (0,0) dan (2,6), berapakah gradiennya?


4. Problem Introduction

4. Pengenalan Masalah

Theory 1: The Core Slope Formula

To find the slope between any two points \( A(x_1, y_1) \) and \( B(x_2, y_2) \), we use: \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \).

Untuk mencari gradien antara dua titik, kita gunakan: m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1).
1. Find the slope of the line passing through \( (2, 4) \) and \( (4, 8) \).
Cari gradien garis yang melalui (2,4) dan (4,8).


2. Calculate the slope for points \( (-1, 2) \) and \( (2, 11) \).
Hitung gradien untuk titik (-1,2) dan (2,11).


3. Find the slope passing through \( (2, 3) \) and \( (6, -1) \).
Cari gradien yang melalui (2,3) dan (6,-1).


Theory 2: Point-Slope Form Equation

Once you have the slope \( m \) and a point \( (x_1, y_1) \), you can build the line's equation using: \( y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \).

Setelah punya gradien m dan titik (x1, y1), kamu bisa membuat persamaan garisnya menggunakan: y - y1 = m(x - x1).
4. If slope \( m = 4 \) and the line passes through \( (1, 2) \), plug these into \( y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \). What replaces \( y_1 \)?
Masukkan ke rumus. Apa pengganti y1?


5. Using the same \( m = 4 \) and point \( (1, 2) \), write the full equation in point-slope form.
Tulis persamaan penuh dalam bentuk titik-gradien.


6. If a line has slope \( m = 3 \) and passes through \( (0, 5) \), what is its equation in point-slope form?
Jika gradien 3 dan melalui (0,5), apa persamaannya?

Theory 3: Distributing to Slope-Intercept Form

We can expand \( y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \) to get \( y = mx + c \). For example, \( y - 2 = 4(x - 1) \) becomes \( y = 4x - 4 + 2 \), which simplifies to \( y = 4x - 2 \).

Kita bisa uraikan y - y1 = m(x - x1) untuk mendapatkan y = mx + c. Contohnya menjadi y = 4x - 2.
7. Expand \( y - 3 = 2(x - 1) \). What is the result after distributing the 2?
Urakan persamaan tersebut. Hasilnya apa setelah dikalikan 2?
8. Finish the equation: \( y - 3 = 2x - 2 \). Move the -3 to the other side. What is \( y \)?
Selesaikan persamaannya. Pindahkan -3. Berapakah y?


9. A line passes through \( (1, 2) \) and \( (3, 10) \). Find the slope \( m \) first.
Garis melalui (1,2) dan (3,10). Cari gradien m dulu.


10. Using the slope from Q9 (\( m=4 \)) and point \( (1, 2) \), convert to \( y = mx + c \) form.
Mengubah ke bentuk y = mx + c.


5. Guided Practice

5. Latihan Terbimbing

Theory 1: Handling Negative Coordinates

When calculating slope with negative numbers, be very careful with signs. For points \( C(2, -1) \) and \( D(4, 7) \):
\( m = \frac{7 - (-1)}{4 - 2} = \frac{7 + 1}{2} = \frac{8}{2} = 4 \).

Saat menghitung gradien dengan angka negatif, berhati-hatilah dengan tanda. Untuk C(2, -1) dan D(4, 7): m = (7 - (-1)) / (4 - 2) = 8/2 = 4.
1. For points \( C(2, -1) \) and \( D(4, 7) \), calculate the numerator (Rise): \( y_2 - y_1 \).
Hitung pembilang (Rise): y2 - y1.


2. For the same points, calculate the denominator (Run): \( x_2 - x_1 \).
Hitung penyebut (Run): x2 - x1.


3. What is the final slope \( m \) of the line passing through \( C(2, -1) \) and \( D(4, 7) \)?
Berapakah gradien akhir m?


Theory 2: Negative Slopes

If a line goes downwards from left to right, its slope is negative. Example: Points \( (1, 4) \) and \( (3, 0) \).
\( m = \frac{0 - 4}{3 - 1} = \frac{-4}{2} = -2 \).

Jika garis turun dari kiri ke kanan, gradiennya negatif. Contoh: m = (0 - 4) / (3 - 1) = -4/2 = -2.
4. Calculate the slope of a line passing through \( (1, 4) \) and \( (3, 0) \).
Hitung gradien garis yang melalui (1,4) dan (3,0).


5. Find the slope passing through \( (-2, 5) \) and \( (1, -1) \).
Cari gradien yang melalui (-2,5) dan (1,-1).


6. If a line has a slope of \( -3 \), does it go up or down from left to right?
Jika gradien -3, apakah garis naik atau turun dari kiri ke kanan?

Theory 3: Parallel Lines

Two lines are parallel if and only if they have the EXACT SAME slope (\( m_1 = m_2 \)). If Line 1 has \( m = 4 \), any line parallel to it also has \( m = 4 \).

Dua garis sejajar jika dan hanya jika mereka punya gradien yang SAMA PERSIS. Jika Garis 1 punya m=4, garis sejajar apa pun dengannya juga punya m=4.
7. True or False: Parallel lines must have different slopes.
Benar atau Salah: Garis sejajar harus punya gradien berbeda.
8. Line A has a slope of 5. Line B is parallel to Line A. What is the slope of Line B?
Garis A gradiennya 5. Garis B sejajar dengan A. Berapa gradien B?


9. A line passes through \( (2, 3) \) and \( (4, 7) \). What is the slope of a line parallel to it?
Garis melalui (2,3) dan (4,7). Berapa gradien garis sejajar dengannya?


10. The equation of a line is \( y = 3x + 5 \). What is the slope of a line parallel to it?
Persamaan garis y = 3x + 5. Berapa gradien garis sejajar dengannya?


6. Independent Practice

6. Latihan Mandiri

Theory 1: Architect Challenge - Building Equations

Given a slope \( m \) and a point \( (x_1, y_1) \), you can construct the equation directly. Example: \( m = -2 \) through \( (0, 3) \).
Equation: \( y - 3 = -2(x - 0) \Rightarrow y = -2x + 3 \).

Diketahui gradien m dan titik (x1, y1), kamu bisa langsung membuat persamaannya. Contoh: y = -2x + 3.
1. Find the equation of the line with slope \( 3 \) passing through \( (1, 2) \). Convert to \( y = mx + c \).
Cari persamaan garis dengan gradien 3 melalui (1,2). Ubah ke y = mx + c.


2. Find the equation of the line with slope \( -1 \) passing through \( (0, 5) \). Convert to \( y = mx + c \).
Cari persamaan garis dengan gradien -1 melalui (0,5).


3. Find the equation of the line with slope \( 4 \) passing through \( (2, 10) \). Convert to \( y = mx + c \).
Cari persamaan garis dengan gradien 4 melalui (2,10).


Theory 2: Finding Slope from Two Points

To find the slope between \( (5, 2) \) and \( (1, 10) \):
\( m = \frac{10 - 2}{1 - 5} = \frac{8}{-4} = -2 \).

Untuk mencari gradien antara (5,2) dan (1,10): m = (10 - 2) / (1 - 5) = 8/-4 = -2.
4. Find the slope of the line passing through \( (5, 2) \) and \( (1, 10) \).
Cari gradien garis melalui (5,2) dan (1,10).


5. Find the slope of the line passing through \( (-1, 3) \) and \( (2, 0) \).
Cari gradien garis melalui (-1,3) dan (2,0).


6. Find the slope of the line passing through \( (2, 5) \) and \( (4, 9) \).
Cari gradien garis melalui (2,5) dan (4,9).


Theory 3: Proving Parallel Lines

Are \( y = 3x + 5 \) and the line through \( (1, 2) \) & \( (2, 5) \) parallel?
Line 1 slope: 3. Line 2 slope: \( m = \frac{5-2}{2-1} = 3 \). Since \( 3 = 3 \), they ARE parallel.

Apakah sejajar? Gradien Garis 1 adalah 3. Gradien Garis 2 adalah 3. Karena 3=3, mereka SEJAJAR.
7. What is the slope of the line \( y = 4x - 2 \)?
Berapakah gradien garis y = 4x - 2?


8. What is the slope of the line passing through \( (1, 2) \) and \( (2, 5) \)?
Berapakah gradien garis yang melalui (1,2) dan (2,5)?


9. Based on Q7 and Q8, is \( y = 3x + 5 \) parallel to the line through \( (1, 2) \) and \( (2, 5) \)?
Berdasarkan jawaban sebelumnya, apakah keduanya sejajar?
10. Find the equation of the line parallel to \( y = 4x - 2 \) that passes through \( (2, 10) \). Write in \( y = mx + c \).
Cari persamaan garis sejajar dengan y = 4x - 2 melalui (2, 10).
