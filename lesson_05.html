Real-World Connection

00:10 - 00:15

Architects must calculate angles perfectly. If a bridge support leans at \(30^\circ\) from the horizontal line, the other side must be \(150^\circ\) to form a straight line!

Arsitek harus menghitung sudut dengan sempurna. Jika penyangga jembatan miring 30° dari garis lurus, sisi satunya pasti 150°!

Quick Quests: Latihan Cepat

Prior Knowledge

00:15 - 00:20

Straight Line (Garis Lurus): Angles on a straight line always add up to \(180^\circ\). Think of a flat ruler!

Sudut pada garis lurus selalu jumlahnya 180°. Bayangkan penggaris yang rata!

Right Angle (Sudut Siku-siku): Formed by perpendicular lines, marked with a small square (\(\square\)), and totals \(90^\circ\). Think of the corner of a book!

Dibentuk oleh garis tegak lurus, ditandai kotak kecil, totalnya 90°. Bayangkan sudut buku!

Straight = 180°

Right = 90°

Problem Introduction

00:20 - 00:30

What if angles are algebra? Because they are Supplementary (Berpelurus), adding them equals \(180^\circ\).

Bagaimana jika sudutnya berbentuk aljabar? Karena berpelurus, jumlahnya pasti 180°.
